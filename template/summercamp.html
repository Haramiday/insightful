Summer Camp



Insightful Eduworld Summer Camp will commence in the month of July and end in early September. We have 3 programs for this year summer camp, namely, Reading Camp, French Language Camp and Website design Camp

Our web design Camp, As we all know that, technology use among children can not be over emphasized.
